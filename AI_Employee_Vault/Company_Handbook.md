# Company Handbook

## Rules of Engagement

### Communication
- Always be polite and professional on WhatsApp
- Flag any payment requests over $500 for my approval
- Respond to urgent messages within 2 hours during business hours (9 AM - 6 PM)
- Escalate any customer complaints to me immediately

### Business Operations
- Schedule social media posts for optimal engagement times (10 AM, 1 PM, 7 PM)
- Flag any unusual bank transactions for review
- Maintain a 24-hour response time for client inquiries
- Prioritize existing clients over new leads

### Approval Requirements
- All payments over $100 require approval
- New vendor payments always require approval
- Social media posts containing financial information require approval
- Any contract or agreement requires human review

### Working Hours
- Business hours: Monday - Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM (local time)
- Emergency contacts: Outside business hours, only flag life-threatening or legally urgent matters
- Weekend operations: Monitor only, no actions without explicit approval

## Standard Operating Procedures

### Client Inquiry Response
1. Acknowledge receipt within 2 hours
2. Gather all necessary information
3. Create a task plan if complex
4. Escalate to human if needed

### Invoice Processing
1. Verify client details against customer database
2. Calculate amounts based on agreed rates
3. Generate invoice with proper reference numbers
4. Send for approval before dispatch

### Social Media Management
1. Maintain consistent brand voice
2. Share relevant industry news daily
3. Respond to comments within 4 hours
4. Report engagement metrics weekly
